Sneaker Care

Welcome to the Sneaker Care page of Foot Fusion, where we share tips and tricks to keep your kicks looking fresh and lasting longer.

Cleaning Guide

Materials:

  • Leather: Wipe gently with a damp cloth and mild soap. Avoid soaking.
  • Suede: Use a soft brush to remove dirt. For stains, use a suede eraser or cleaner.
  • Canvas: Hand wash with warm water and gentle detergent.
  • Mesh: Use a toothbrush and soapy water to clean delicate areas.

Steps:

  1. Remove laces and insoles.
  2. Dust off loose dirt.
  3. Use the appropriate cleaning method based on the material.
  4. Air dry in a shaded area.

Storage Tips

  • Store sneakers in a cool, dry place.
  • Use shoe trees to maintain shape.
  • Avoid direct sunlight and damp environments.
  • Keep original boxes or use breathable shoe storage bags.

Products You Need

  • Soft brushes: For gentle scrubbing.
  • Mild cleaning solutions: Designed for sneakers.
  • Suede erasers: For delicate materials.
  • Odor eliminators: Like charcoal bags or sprays.
  • Waterproof sprays: To protect against stains and water damage.

DIY Hacks

  • Remove scuffs with a white eraser or toothpaste.
  • Absorb odors by placing baking soda inside the shoes overnight.
  • Use a hairdryer (on cool setting) to remove small wrinkles in leather.

Do's and Don'ts

Do:

  • Clean stains as soon as possible.
  • Rotate your sneakers to extend their lifespan.
  • Use gentle motions to avoid damaging materials.

Don't:

  • Machine wash unless the brand specifies it\u2019s safe.
  • Use harsh chemicals like bleach.
  • Leave sneakers in extreme temperatures.
